# 🏠 HomeZayka — Authentic Home-Cooked Meals

**HomeZayka** is a community-driven platform designed to connect talented local homemakers with food enthusiasts seeking fresh, healthy, and authentic home-cooked meals. No commercial kitchens, no mass production—just real food made with love.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🥗 For Customers
* **Browse Local Talent:** Discover diverse cuisines from verified home cooks in your neighborhood.
* **Smart Filtering:** Sort by **Recommended**, **Price**, or **Rating** to find exactly what you're craving.
* **Seamless Booking:** Select pickup time slots and portions with a high-fidelity checkout flow.
* **Personal Dashboard:** Track active orders (Pending/Confirmed) and view your order history.

### 👨‍🍳 For Cooks
* **Kitchen Dashboard:** A dedicated space to manage incoming requests in real-time.
* **Order Control:** **Accept** or **Reject** orders based on your kitchen's capacity.
* **Earnings Tracking:** View potential payouts for every confirmed order.
* **Profile Management:** Showcase your bio, specialties, and **Verified** status to build trust.

---

## 🎨 Design & UX

The project follows a **"Warm & Professional"** aesthetic:
* **Typography:** Elegant Serif (`font-display`) for headings; clean Sans-serif (`Inter/Geist`) for body text.
* **Palette:** Warm White (`#F9F8F4`), Mustard Action Buttons (`#E2A856`), and Basil Green status indicators.
* **Interactions:** Smooth Framer Motion animations for tab switching and page transitions.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** React.js (Vite)
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
* **Routing:** React Router DOM (v6)
* **Icons:** FontAwesome 6 (Solid & Regular)
* **State Management:** React Context API (`AppProvider`)
